About Us

Our Team

The Lake Life is a group of friends who water-ski on Webster Lake, snow-ski together in the winter, and are ready to do anything.The Lake Life waterskis at Nipmuc Waterski Club in Webster, Massachusetts.

The Lake Life consists of Liam Menard, Evan Fallon, Jimmy Kokernak, and Wil Dems.

Our Youtube

Wil Dems

Wil Dems is a Junior at St. John's, he waterskis at Nipmuc Waterski Club and is a member of the St. John's Ski Race Team

Liam Menard

Liam Menard, a sophomore at Whitinsville Christian School, designs webpages for his school newspaper, water-skis for Nipmuc Water Ski Club, and snow skis for Ski Ward Race Team

Evan Fallon

Evan Fallon, a junior at Westborough High School, is an impressive water-skier at Nimpuc Waterski Club and competes at Show Ski Tournaments. He also snow skis for Ski Ward Race Team.

Jimmy Kokernak

Jimmy Kokernak, a sophomore at St. Johns, is a competitive waterski jumper for Nipmuc waterski club. He hopes to jump 100 feet this year.
