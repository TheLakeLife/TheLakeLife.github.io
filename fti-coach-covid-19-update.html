FTI COACH – COVID-19 Update

Apr 09, 2022

First, I want to let you know we are here for you during this crisis.

We will have an agent available from 9am to 2pm Monday Through Friday by phone or email and our 24 hour emergency line is still active for all current clients.

To help curb the spread of the Covid Virus, our office will only be open to employees or by appointment.

We would like to take this time to say THANK YOU to our clients who have supported us for the past 35 years. Foxy Travel and FTI Coach remain in a good financial position to weather these historic times, and look forward to serving our community for years to come.

Please know that when you are ready to travel, and it is responsible to do so, Foxy Travel and FTI Coach Lines is ready to work for you.
