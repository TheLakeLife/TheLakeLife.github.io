The Lake Life is a group of friends who water-ski on Webster Lake, snow-ski together in the winter, and are ready to do anything.
The Lake Life waterskis at Nipmuc Waterski Club in Webster, Massachusetts.
The Lake Life consists of Liam Menard, Evan Fallon, Jimmy Kokernak, and Wil Dems.
